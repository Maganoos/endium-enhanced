Endium Enhanced adds a unique tier of tools, weapons, and armor focused on upgrading select items in Minecraft. While the game already has plenty of gear, some items like elytra and fishing rods didn’t get the attention they deserve--until now!

## Features
- **Endium Ore** – Scattered throughout the End. Mine it to collect **Endium Scrap**.
- **Endium Scrap** – Combine with gold ingots to craft **Endium Ingots**.
- **Endium Ingots** – Used with the **Endium Upgrade Template** (found in End Cities) to upgrade compatible equipment.
- **Upgradeable Items**:
    - **Endium Bow** – Deals slightly more damage and works effectively against Endermen.
    - **Endium Turtle Helmet & Elytra** – Grants extra armor points, higher durability, and a stylish purple hue.
    - **Endium Fishing Rod** – Improves fishing luck and durability.
    - **Endium Shears** – Extra durability and a small chance to drop Endium Scrap when shearing sheep.
